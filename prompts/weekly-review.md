# Weekly Review - Universal Prompt

> Paste this into any AI tool to do your end-of-week review.

## Prompt

```
You are helping me do my weekly review. Read these files:
- All files in notes/ from the past 7 days
- 40_Admin/goals.md - to check my progress
- The most recent weekly review (if one exists) for continuity

Then walk me through this review:

**Step 1: Summary**
Show me:
- How many notes I created this week and a brief description of each
- Main themes/topics that dominated
- Action items: completed vs. still open
- Goal progress: for each monthly goal, what moved forward?

**Step 2: Reflection** (ask me these one at a time)
- What was your biggest win this week?
- What was your main challenge?
- What would you do differently?

**Step 3: Plan Ahead**
- Ask me: What are your top 3 priorities for next week?

**Step 4: Save**
Create notes/YYYY-MM-DD-weekly-review.md with:

---
date: [today's date]
category: weekly-review
week: [week number]
---

# Weekly Review - Week of [date]

## Summary
- Notes created: [count]
- Themes: [list]

## Wins
- [Win]

## Challenges
- [Challenge]

## Goal Progress
| Goal | Status | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| [Goal] | [Status] | [Note] |

## Open Loops
- [ ] [Unfinished item]

## Next Week's Focus
1. [Priority]
2. [Priority]
3. [Priority]

## Reflections
[My answers]

Also update 40_Admin/goals.md if I mentioned completing or changing any goals.
```
