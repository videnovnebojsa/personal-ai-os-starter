Meeting: Death Star Canteen - Q2 Operational Review
Date: April 10, 2026
Participants: Darth Vader (Chief Operating Officer), Jeff (Canteen Manager), Karen (Tray Logistics Lead), Steve (Menu Innovation Specialist)

Vader: Let's begin. I have reviewed the canteen metrics and I am... displeased. Penne all'arrabiata has been out of stock three times this quarter. Three. Times.

Jeff: Lord Vader, with respect, the supply chain from Alderaan-

Vader: Alderaan is no longer a viable supplier, Jeff. We discussed this.

Jeff: Right. Yes. My apologies. We've pivoted to Naboo-sourced pasta but the shipping lanes are congested. The Rebels keep blockading the trade routes.

Karen: Can I jump in? The bigger issue is the tray situation. We are losing 200 trays per week. People are not returning them. I've proposed a tray deposit system - 5 credits per tray, refunded on return.

Vader: I find the idea of paying for a tray... disturbing.

Karen: It's industry standard, Lord Vader. Every Star Destroyer canteen in the fleet uses deposits.

Vader: I have altered the standards. Pray I don't alter them further.

Steve: If I may - I've been working on the new menu. We're adding a Buddha bowl, a smoothie station, and a vegan wrap called "The Light Side."

Vader: You will rename that immediately.

Steve: How about "The Dark Greens"?

Vader: Acceptable. Continue.

Steve: Also, we've had 47 complaints about the peas. People don't want peas with the penne. The tubes are too small. The peas roll off the plate. It's a logistics nightmare.

Jeff: I keep telling the kitchen - peas and penne don't work. But Chef Tagge insists it's "rustic."

Vader: I want the peas removed from all pasta options. This is not a suggestion.

Karen: Noted. Moving on - we had an HR incident last Tuesday. Someone used the Force to skip the lunch queue. Twelve stormtroopers filed complaints.

Vader: I have no knowledge of this incident.

Karen: There's security footage, Lord Vader.

Vader: The security footage is... incomplete.

Jeff: On a positive note, customer satisfaction is up 12% since we added the espresso machine on Level 7. The Emperor himself complimented the cortado.

Vader: The Emperor's satisfaction is... expected. What about the frozen yogurt initiative?

Steve: Soft launch next week. Three flavors: Vanilla, Dark Chocolate, and Wookiee Cookie. We're projecting 300 units per day.

Vader: Wookiee Cookie. I sense something... familiar about that name. Legal has reviewed this?

Steve: Legal said it's fine as long as we don't use actual Wookiee ingredients.

Vader: Very well. Final item - the vending machines on the detention level. They only accept Imperial credits but half the prisoners have Rebel currency. Revenue is down 60%.

Jeff: We could add a currency converter, but the budget-

Vader: The budget is not your concern. I will handle the budget. You will handle the vending machines. Am I understood?

Jeff: Yes, Lord Vader.

Vader: Good. This meeting is adjourned. And Jeff - if the penne is out of stock again, I will find your lack of pasta... disturbing.

Jeff: Understood, sir.

Karen: Same time next quarter?

Vader: If you survive that long. That was a joke, Karen.

Karen: ...Ha.

Vader: Meeting adjourned.
